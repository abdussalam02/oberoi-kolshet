Overview

Discover Elevated Living at Kolshet's Finest Address - Oberoi Realty, Where Dreams Take Shape!

Oberoi Kolshet Road Thane offers a collection of luxurious smart homes within a gated community. Situated in a lush green area, this enclave provides an exclusive and tranquil living experience, making it an ideal choice for those seeking to escape the daily grind of urban life.

Oberoi Realty presents its latest masterpiece, the luxury segment of Kolshet Road Thane, featuring an unparalleled range of premium apartments, including 2 BHK, 3 BHK, and 4 BHK options. With Oberoi's renowned commitment to quality and exceptional facilities, Oberoi Kolshet Road sets the standard for opulence. This residential property presents a unique opportunity to embrace a luxurious lifestyle early on. Residents can enjoy the convenience of a fully equipped club with various recreational amenities, such as a swimming pool, gymnasium, health club, indoor badminton court, party halls, billiards, table tennis, board games, reading room, and even a crèche. The project spans across 18 acres of land.
